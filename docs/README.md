## Key Personas

### Maya — The "Suburban Sanctuary Seeker"
- 32 years old, first-time homeowner, Philadelphia suburb
- Wants a "Pinterest-ready" outdoor space
- Pain: Overwhelmed by hardiness zones, forgets watering schedules

### Liam — The "Health-Conscious Harvester"
- 38 years old, teacher in Austin, TX
- Wants to grow food with his kids
- Pain: Pest identification, succession planting knowledge

## MVP Priority Features (P0)

1. **Third-Party Plant ID** — AI-powered plant species identification (>90% accuracy)
2. **Weather-Synced Calendar** — Maintenance schedule that adapts to local weather
3. **User Profile & Garden DB** — Cloud storage for plant inventory

## Target Market

- Initial segment: Suburban Newbie Homeowner
- TAM: $2.88B (72M gardening households × $40 annual subscription)
- Fastest growing: Urban "Small Space" Gardeners (13.5% CAGR)

## Monetization

- **Free Tier**: Unlimited basic ID scans, standard weather alerts, ad-supported
- **Premium Tier**: Ad-free, Expert Human Review credits, Advanced AI features
